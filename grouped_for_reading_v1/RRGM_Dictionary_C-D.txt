===== C =====

C001 can


Common meaning:
To be able to do something, or to have permission to do something.


Example:
She can lift the box.


RRGM bridge:
Can marks capacity or permission. These are not the same: a system may be able to act but still lack valid permission to act.

C002 cannot


Common meaning:
To be unable to do something, or not permitted to do something.


Example:

RRGM bridge:
Cannot marks a closed path. The closure may come from lack of capacity, lack of permission, boundary, rule, or failed courier.

C003 could


Common meaning:
