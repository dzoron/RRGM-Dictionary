ED001 embedding-space

Common meaning:
A numerical space in which encoded items are located by learned relation.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Embedding-space is a digital coordinate field where semantic or structural proximity is represented as distance or direction.

Active domains:
[DIG]

Domain entry:
[DIG] Tokens, images, records, or concepts occupy positions produced by an embedding model.

Count condition:
Counts when a model maps inputs to vectors in a declared dimensional space.

Specific transfer boundary:
Proximity in embedding-space does not by itself prove human-equivalent meaning.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
