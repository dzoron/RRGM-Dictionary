OP001 oscillation

Common meaning:
Repeated variation around a reference or equilibrium state.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Oscillation is physical recurrence in which a measurable quantity moves back and forth through a range.

Active domains:
[PHY]

Domain entry:
[PHY] Period, amplitude, phase, and damping describe how the recurrence behaves.

Count condition:
Counts when the quantity repeatedly changes direction or sign around a reference state.

Specific transfer boundary:
Not every repetition is sinusoidal or mechanically periodic.

[DEF]
