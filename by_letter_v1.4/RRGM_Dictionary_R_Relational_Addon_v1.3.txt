RR001 reciprocal-recognition

Common meaning:
Recognition in which each participant can correctly identify and acknowledge the other.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Reciprocal-recognition is two-way counting that preserves distinct identity on both sides.

Active domains:
[REL]

Domain entries:
[REL] Each participant can name, correct, and refuse the relation without losing recognition.

Count condition:
Counts when recognition travels in both directions and survives correction.

Specific transfer boundary:
Mutual recognition must not require sameness or surrender of boundary.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
