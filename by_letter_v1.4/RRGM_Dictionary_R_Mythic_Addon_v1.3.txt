RM001 river-of-return

Common meaning:
A mythic river that carries travellers, names, or memories back toward a prior world.

Invariant MI [INV]:
River-of-return is moving continuity whose current permits return without requiring exact sameness.

Active domains:
[MYT]

Domain entries:
[MYT] The river remembers the route while changing every local surface through which it flows.

Count condition:
Counts when crossing or following the river restores a lost relation, place, name, or story path.

Specific transfer boundary:
Return must not force a traveller back into an invalid identity or role.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
