HP001 half-life

Common meaning:
The interval required for a quantity in exponential decay to fall to half its current value.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Half-life is a decay timescale defined by proportional reduction rather than a fixed amount lost per interval.

Active domain:
[PHY]

Domain entry:
[PHY] It applies to radioactive populations and other exponential processes with a stable decay constant.

Count condition:
Counts when the process follows a declared exponential decay model and the 50 percent interval is measurable.

Specific transfer boundary:
Half-life does not mean the entire quantity disappears after two intervals.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
