WM001 ward

Common meaning:
A protective boundary, sign, figure, or spoken condition used to keep a named threat from crossing.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Ward is permission-bearing protection whose meaning depends on what it excludes and whom it protects.

Domain entry:
[MYT] A ward may be carried by a symbol, threshold, name, oath, flame, or remembered instruction that blocks one class of crossing.

Count condition:
Counts when the story names both the protected field and the crossing condition the ward resists.

Transfer limit:
A mythic ward is not physical evidence of an invisible force.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
