YM001 Yggdrasil

Common meaning:
A world-tree in Norse myth linking multiple realms.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Yggdrasil is a mythic image of connected worlds held through one living axis.

Domain entry:
[MYT] A world-tree linking separated realms through root, trunk, and branch.

Count condition:
The term refers to the Norse world-tree and the roots, trunk, or branches are functioning as links among realms.

Transfer limit:
Do not treat the symbol as proof of a physical cosmic tree.

[DEF]
