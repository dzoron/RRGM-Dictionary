RRGM Dictionary G Biological Addon v0.8

Scope: New G-terms whose primary implementation belongs to the [BIO] domain and which are not present as headwords in the base G section.
Production rule: Each entry carries its own invariant MI. Definitions are compressed, domain-specific, and proof remains local.

Entry count: 24

GB001 gamete

Common meaning:
A reproductive cell that can combine with another gamete to form a zygote.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Gamete is a haploid biological carrier contributing one parental genome set to a new organism.

Active domain:
[BIO]

Domain entry:
[BIO] Sperm and egg are common specialized gametes in animals; other organisms use different forms.

Count condition:
Counts when the cell is developmentally capable of participating in fertilization under the species conditions.

Specific transfer boundary:
Gamete status does not establish personhood or legal status.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
