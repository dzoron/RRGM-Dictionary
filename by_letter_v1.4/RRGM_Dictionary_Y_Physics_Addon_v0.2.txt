YP001 yaw

Common meaning:
Rotation of a body around its vertical axis.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Yaw is orientation change around the vertical reference axis.

Domain entry:
[PHY] Rotation that turns a body left or right around its vertical axis.

Count condition:
A body changes heading through rotation about a declared vertical axis.

Transfer limit:
The active axis and reference frame must be declared.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]

YP002 yaw-angle

Common meaning:
The angular amount of yaw rotation.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Yaw-angle measures horizontal orientation relative to a reference direction.

Domain entry:
[PHY] The angle between a body's heading and a declared reference heading.

Count condition:
A yaw reference direction and current heading are declared so their angular difference can be measured.

Transfer limit:
Do not confuse yaw with pitch or roll.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]

YP003 yaw-rate

Invariant MI [INV]:
Yaw-rate measures rotational change around the vertical axis.

Domain entry:
[PHY] Change in yaw angle per unit measured time.

Count condition:
Yaw angle is measured at more than one point in time so its rate of change can be determined.

Transfer limit:
The sign convention and frame must be declared.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]

YP004 Youngs-modulus

Common meaning:
A measure of elastic stiffness under axial stress.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Youngs-modulus relates stress to strain in the linear elastic region.

Domain entry:
[PHY] The ratio of axial stress to axial strain while material response remains approximately linear and elastic.

Count condition:
Axial stress and strain are measured in a material region where response is approximately linear and elastic.

Transfer limit:
It does not describe plastic deformation beyond the elastic region.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
