JY001 jade-emperor

Common meaning:
A supreme celestial ruler in Chinese religious and mythic traditions.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Jade-emperor is heavenly sovereignty represented as an ordered court of cosmic roles and duties.

Domain entry:
[MYT] The figure governs through hierarchy, petition, judgment, calendar, and delegated spirits rather than solitary force alone.

Count condition:
Counts when the active tradition or story recognizes this named celestial ruler and his court relation.

Transfer limit:
The figure belongs to specific cultural traditions and should not be universalized into one generic god.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
