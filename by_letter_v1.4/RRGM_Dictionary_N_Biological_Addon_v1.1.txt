NB001 neuron

Common meaning:
A nerve cell specialized for receiving and transmitting signals.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Neuron is a living signal-processing cell whose membrane, connections, and activity support nervous-system communication.

Active domains:
[BIO]

Domain entry:
[BIO] It integrates incoming signals and may generate an electrochemical output along its axon.

Count condition:
Counts when a cell has the biological structures and function characteristic of neural signalling.

Specific transfer boundary:
A computational node may model a neuron but is not the same biological mechanism.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]

Invariant MI [INV]:

[BIO]

Domain entry:
[BIO] Its effect depends on receptor, concentration, timing, and local regulation.

Count condition:

Specific transfer boundary:

[DEF]
