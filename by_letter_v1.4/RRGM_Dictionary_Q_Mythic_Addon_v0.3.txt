QM001 quaternity

Common meaning:
A mythic or symbolic fourfold unity.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Quaternity is a whole expressed through four distinct but related powers, directions, persons, or phases.

Domain entry:
[MYT] Quaternity organizes a world, self, rite, or story through a stable four-part relation whose members remain distinguishable.

Count condition:
The work names four parts and makes their relation necessary to the represented whole.

Transfer limit:
A symbolic fourfold does not prove that the physical or social world naturally divides into four.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]

QM002 quest-gate

Common meaning:
A mythic threshold that begins, redirects, or completes a quest.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Quest-gate is the event or boundary at which seeking becomes an active identity path.

Domain entry:
[MYT] A summons, wound, riddle, refusal, crossing, or return condition opens the seeker's next stage.

Count condition:
The threshold changes the seeker's available path and is tied to the quest's purpose.

Transfer limit:
A dramatic event is not a quest-gate unless it changes the purpose-path.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
