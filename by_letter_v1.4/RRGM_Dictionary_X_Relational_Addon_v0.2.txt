XR001 xenia

Common meaning:
Hospitality, guest-friendship, or protected relation between host and stranger.

Invariant MI [INV]:
A stranger relation is recognized through reciprocal duties rather than threat by default.

Domain entry:
[REL] A bounded host-guest relation built from welcome, protection, reciprocity, and respect.

Example:
The village practiced xenia by offering food, names, and a clear exit path.

Coupler:
Recognition couples host duty, guest boundary, reciprocity, and local rule.

Count condition:
Both welcome and refusal can count, and neither party loses dignity.

Non-example / transfer limit:
Unrestricted access is not xenia.
