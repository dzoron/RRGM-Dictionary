XM001 x-crossroads

Common meaning:
An X-shaped point where paths divide or meet in mythic narrative.

Invariant MI [INV]:
A marked convergence where more than one possible path remains active.

Domain entry:
[MYT] A decision-place where identity, fate, duty, or return paths become visible together.

Example:
The wanderer reached the x-crossroads and had to choose which promise to keep.

Coupler:
Narrative position couples path, identity pressure, and symbolic consequence.

Count condition:
At least two meaningful paths meet and the choice changes the later story.

Non-example / transfer limit:
A literal road crossing is [PHY] unless a mythic mapping is declared.
