VM001 veil

Common meaning:
A symbolic covering that hides, separates, or protects what lies beyond ordinary sight.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Veil is a mythic boundary that limits recognition without proving absence.

Domain entry:
[MYT] Veil can divide worlds, conceal a name, protect a sacred presence, or delay revelation until a passage condition is met.

Count condition:
Counts when concealment has narrative force and crossing or lifting it changes recognition.

Transfer limit:
A veil is symbolic structure, not physical proof of another realm.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
