DD001 data-lineage

Common meaning:
The trace of where data came from and how it changed.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Data-lineage is the ordered ancestry of a dataset across collection, transformation, copying, and use.

Active domains:
[DIG]

Domain entry:
[DIG] It links each current field to source records, processing steps, and responsible systems.

Count condition:
Counts when a current value can be traced through its material transformations to one or more sources.

Specific transfer boundary:
Lineage does not prove source accuracy.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
