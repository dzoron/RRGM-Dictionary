RRGM Dictionary L Digital Addon v0.7

Scope: New L-terms whose primary implementation belongs to the [DIG] domain and which are not present as headwords in the base L section.
Production rule: Each entry carries its own invariant MI. Definitions are compressed, domain-specific, and proof remains local.

Entry count: 24

LD001 latency

Common meaning:
Delay between an input, request, or event and the resulting response.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Latency is elapsed processing or transmission delay that changes when an output becomes available.

Active domain:
[DIG]

Domain entry:
[DIG] It may arise in networks, storage, model inference, queues, or user interfaces.

Count condition:
Counts when both a triggering event and a measurable response time are declared.

Specific transfer boundary:
Latency is delay, not necessarily failure or low throughput.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
