RRGM Dictionary B Digital Addon v0.8

Scope: New B-terms whose primary implementation belongs to the [DIG] domain and which are not present as headwords in the base B section.
Production rule: Each entry carries its own invariant MI. Definitions are compressed, domain-specific, and proof remains local.

Entry count: 24

BD001 backend

Common meaning:
The server-side or internal processing layer behind a user-facing system.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Backend is the hidden computational region that stores state, applies rules, and returns results to an interface.

Active domain:
[DIG]

Domain entry:
[DIG] It may contain services, databases, queues, authentication, and business logic.

Count condition:
Counts when the layer performs non-interface processing required by the application.

Specific transfer boundary:
Backend does not mean unobservable, secure, or authoritative by default.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
