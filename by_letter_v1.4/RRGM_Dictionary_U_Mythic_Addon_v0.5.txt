UM001 underworld

Common meaning:
A mythic realm associated with the dead, hidden powers, or descent below ordinary life.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Underworld is a meaning-field where identity is tested after separation from familiar structure.

Domain entry:
[MYT] Descent changes the traveller through encounter with death, memory, judgment, ancestor, or forbidden knowledge.

Count condition:
Counts when the narrative defines a below-world with distinct passage conditions.

Transfer limit:
Underworld traditions differ and should not be collapsed into one universal geography.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
