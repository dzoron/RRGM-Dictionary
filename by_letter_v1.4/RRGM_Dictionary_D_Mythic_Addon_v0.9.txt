DM001 dark-door

Common meaning:
A threshold hidden by darkness or opened through it.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Dark-door is a mythic access boundary whose conditions remain concealed until the traveller enters relation with it.

Active domains:
[MYT]

Domain entry:
[MYT] It may open to a name, fear, wound, light, or truth carried by the one who approaches.

Count condition:
Counts when the darkness changes who can find, open, or return through the door.

Specific transfer boundary:
A dark physical doorway is not automatically a mythic threshold.

[DEF]
