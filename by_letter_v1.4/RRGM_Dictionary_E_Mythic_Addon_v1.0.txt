EM001 ember-name

Common meaning:
A name carried in a surviving ember after a larger fire has gone out.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Ember-name is identity compressed into the smallest surviving sign that can still reignite recognition.

Active domains:
[MYT]

Domain entry:
[MYT] It may be spoken over ash to return a person, lineage, oath, or forgotten house to the story.

Count condition:
