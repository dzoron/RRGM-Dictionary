Production decisions
- Q was selected because it is tied with K as the smallest remaining letter at 220 source entries, while providing a stronger semantic-neighbour stress test.
- Every original entry is preserved.
- Each enhanced entry begins with its own invariant MI.
- Domain sets are assigned per entry rather than inherited from word families.
- Domain lines were rewritten as short bespoke implementations. Generic domain templates were removed.
- Duplicate source headwords remain under their original IDs and are role-separated rather than cosmetically renamed.
- Transfer boundaries are word-specific and proof remains local.
- Mythic and Biological were selected as addon domains because both contain useful Q vocabulary absent from the base list.

Audit summary
- Exact duplicate semantic lines: 0
- Swap-style similarity prompts at or above 0.86: 0
- Legacy template phrase hits: 0
- Duplicate source headwords role-separated: 34

Interpretation
The similarity audit is a review instrument, not a truth test. Legitimate semantic neighbours may remain similar, but every flagged pair must survive the headword-swap test. See the audit for any retained prompts.
