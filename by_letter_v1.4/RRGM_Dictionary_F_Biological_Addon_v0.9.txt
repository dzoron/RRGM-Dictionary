FB001 fascia

Common meaning:
Sheets and bands of connective tissue surrounding and linking body structures.

Invariant MI [INV]:
Fascia is continuous connective ES that supports separation, transmission of force, and movement among tissues.

Active domains:
[BIO]

Domain entry:
[BIO] It surrounds muscles, organs, vessels, and other structures while allowing coordinated motion.

Count condition:
Counts when collagen-rich connective tissue forms an identifiable enclosing or linking layer.

Specific transfer boundary:
Fascia is not a universal explanation for all pain.

Epistemic status:
[DEF]
